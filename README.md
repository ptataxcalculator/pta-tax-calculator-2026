## 📊 Why Use This Calculator?
Mobile registration taxes in Pakistan are calculated based on several factors:
1. **Customs Duty**
2. **Regulatory Duty (RD)**
3. **Sales Tax (Fixed/Ad Valorem)**
4. **Mobile Levy**
5. **Income Tax (Withholding Tax)**

Our tool automates this complex formula, providing you with a total amount in seconds.

## 🛠 How to Register Your Phone
1. Find your **IMEI** by dialing `*#06#`.
2. Visit [PTA Tax Check](https://ptataxcheck.pk/pta-tax-check-calculator/) to estimate your payable amount.
3. Dial `*8484#` or visit the [PTA DRS Portal](https://dirbs.pta.gov.pk/drs) to generate your PSID.
4. Pay your tax via ATM, Online Banking, or any EasyPaisa/JazzCash shop.
